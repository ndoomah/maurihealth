About

MauriHealth is an upcoming real time system that will detect infectious diseases outbreaks throughout different regions found in Mauritius.

This portal is where users can give their input on what diseases they may have contracted and when. This information will help for the system to detect these outbreaks earlier. The user will enter their information anonymously.

Contribute to the system by giving disease information below.

Gastroenteritis
